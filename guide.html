Essential Information

Visa Requirements

Visa policies vary by country. Most destinations now offer e-Visas or Visa-on-Arrival for tourists. Always check the official government portal 3 months before travel.

Packing Essentials

  • Universal travel adapter
  • Power bank (10,000mAh+)
  • Lightweight rain jacket
  • Copy of important documents

Budgeting Tips

Use local currency for better rates. Avoid currency exchange at airports. Use apps like Revolut or Wise for low-fee withdrawals.
